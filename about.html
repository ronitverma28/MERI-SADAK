About MERI SADAK

Building better roads through community participation and transparent governance

Our Mission

MERI SADAK is a citizen-centric platform designed to bridge the gap between community needs and infrastructure maintenance.

We believe that every citizen has the right to safe, well-maintained roads, and every voice matters in building better infrastructure. Our platform empowers communities to report issues, track progress, and hold authorities accountable for road maintenance.

Through technology and transparency, we're creating a more responsive and efficient system that puts citizens at the heart of infrastructure development.

Our Vision

To create a world where every road is safe, every complaint is heard, and every community thrives.

We envision a future where technology serves as a bridge between citizens and government, enabling real-time communication, transparent processes, and efficient problem-solving.

Our goal is to make road maintenance a collaborative effort where every stakeholder - from individual citizens to government agencies - works together for the common good.

How It Works

Simple, transparent, and effective - here's how our platform works

1. Report Issue

Citizens can easily report road maintenance issues through our user-friendly interface. Simply describe the problem, add photos, and specify the location.

2. Review & Assign

Our team reviews each complaint, categorizes it by priority and type, then assigns it to the appropriate maintenance department for action.

3. Track Progress

Citizens can track the status of their complaints in real-time, receive updates, and see when issues are resolved with before/after photos.
